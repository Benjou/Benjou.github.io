Below are plots for various draininage basins contributing to the Thames

as well as the Thames itself.
For each plot, the Greener the line, the more upstream the measuring station; the reder, the more downstream (i.e. close to South Hinksey).
Minns Estate (by the Seacourt P&R) is added in all charts as it is the closest to South Hinksey.
All charts are interactive and zoomable.
You can display and hide any measuring station by clicking its name in the legend on the right.
